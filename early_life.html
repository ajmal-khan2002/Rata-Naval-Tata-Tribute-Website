Early Life & Education

The Founder of Tata Groups

Birth and Family Background:

He was born into the prestigious Tata family, a prominent name in Indian industry and philanthropy. He is the great-grandson of Jamsetji Tata, the founder of the Tata Group, India’s biggest conglomerate. His father, Naval Tata, was adopted by the Tata family and played a significant role in Tata Group's history. Ratan Tata's mother, Sooni Tata, and father separated when he was ten years old.

Childhood:

Ratan Tata and his younger brother, Noel Tata, were raised by their grandmother, Lady Navajbai Tata, after their parents' divorce. His early years were marked by the guidance and values instilled by his grandmother, which had a profound impact on his character and future endeavors.

Education:

Primary and Secondary Education:
Attended Campion School in Mumbai until the 8th grade.
Completed secondary education at Cathedral and John Connon School in Mumbai.
Also attended Bishop Cotton School in Shimla.
Undergraduate Education:
Bachelor’s degree in Architecture with a specialization in Structural Engineering from Cornell University, USA (Class of 1962).
Postgraduate Education:
Completed the Advanced Management Program at Harvard Business School, USA (Class of 1975).
